An/m (verb particle)

Positive interrogative form of copula

Subject is (emphatic) personal pronoun

  • An tusa Iain? ‘Are you John?’
  • An ise Peigi? ‘Is she Peggy?’
  • An iadsan na tidsearan? ‘Are they the teachers?’
  • An esan do athair? ‘Is he your father?’

Subject is definite noun phrase

  • An i/e Anna an tidsear? ‘Is Ann the teacher?’
  • An e do athair am ministear? ‘Is your father the minister?’
  • An e an sagart bhur bhràthair? ‘Is the priest your brother?’
  • An e seo an tidsear? ‘Is this the teacher?’
  • An i/e siud Màiri? ‘Is that Mary?’

cf.

  • An e thusa Iain? ‘Are you John?’
  • An e ise Peigi? ‘Is she Peggy?’
  • An e iadsan na tidsearan? ‘Are they the teachers?’
  • An e esan do athair? ‘Is he your father?’

Takes dependent form of verb

  • A bheil Màiri fuar? ‘Is Mary cold?’
  • A bheil airgead agad? ‘Do you have (any) money?’
