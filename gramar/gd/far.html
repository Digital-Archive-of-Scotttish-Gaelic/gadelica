Far

Fàg e far an d’fhuair thu e.
‘Leave it where you got it.’

Lorg sinn àite far an gabhamaid anail.
‘We found a place where we could draw breath.’

Chunnaic mi far an do chuir thu e.
‘I saw where you put it.’

An aithne dhut an t-àite far a bheil Anna a’ fuireach?
‘Do you know the place where Ann lives?’
