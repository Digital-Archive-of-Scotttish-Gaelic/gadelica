beithe n.

  • beithe n.f.
  • gen. sing. beithe
  • pl. beithean
  1. the birch tree
  2. the wood of the birch tree
[eDIL beithe]

forms

This word displays gender variation; the evidence indicates that it is chiefly feminine in the modern period but frequently masculine in earlier evidence.
